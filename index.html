Conference paper: How aerodynamics affect the design of the aircraft sturcture

Abstract

Since the invention of the first aeroplane many engineers have been finding ways on how we can improve the structure on aeroplanes and aircrafts in general. This paper is designed to look into what discoveries some engineers have made and what the result of using these concept ideas were. The paper is divided into 4 main sections : drag , weight, thrust and lift. In each section , an idea has been covered on how we could change the structure of an aircraft, like making the wings on a plane more angled to get a life force from the plane.
